Anna Hill's Biography

Hi! My name is Anna Hill. Anna was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. Anna has an amazing family comprised of two loving parents, two amazing siblings, and one cuddly dog.

Anna is double majoring in Advertising with an emphasis in Production and Information Management & Technology with a concentration in Web Design. One day, she hopes to become a producer on television commercials. Anna is also extremely organized, determined, and positive.

Outside of work, Anna enjoys the following activities:

  • Baking
  • Volunteering
  • Spending time with family and friends

Personal Information

Anna grew up working in the entertainment industry as an actor and model. Anna has had the opportunity to star in numerous commercials and print advertisements. Now, Anna plans to use her Advertising Production Degree from Syracuse University to work on the other side of the lens as a Producer.
